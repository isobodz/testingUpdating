Strength, Community, and Health: Welcome to Wakefield's Isobodz Gym!

Whether you're taking your first steps into the gym or are seasoned bodybuilder, our doors are open to everyone.

For Women

We believe fitness should be accessible and unintimidating. Our supportive environment and tailored programs empower women to embrace strength and confidence.

For Bodybuilders/Powerlifters

Equipped with free weights, dumbbells, and all essential machines, our gym is a heaven for those seeking to push their limits.

For General Fitness Enthusiasts

From yoga spaces to boxing bags and strongman tires, we offer a diverse range of facilities to suit every fitness journey.

Ready to join our fitness family? Contact us or drop by for a tour. Your journey to strength and wellness starts here.

Our Services

PT and Nutrition Advice

Our PT and nutrition advice is more than a workout plan; it's a roadmap to success. Catering to women's unique needs, we provide the tools and encouragement to thrive.

Gym amenities

Explore our gym's wide array of equipment and facilities. With options for off-peak, regular, and 24/7 access, we provide the flexibility and community to fuel your fitness passion.

Deep-Tissue Massage

Revitalize your body with deep-tissue massage therapy, tailored to your individual needs. Whether you're an athlete or seeking relaxation, we're here to support your wellness journey.
